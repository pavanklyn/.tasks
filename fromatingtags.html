My Hobby: Playing Cricket

Cricket is a popular outdoor team sport played between two teams of eleven players each. It is known for its exciting gameplay, strategic thinking, and team coordination.

The game is played with a bat and ball on a large field, centered around a 22-yard-long pitch. One team bats while the other bowls and fields. The aim of the batting team is to score runs, while the bowling team's goal is to dismiss the batters.

There are different formats of cricket such as Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20), each with its own rules and excitement.

I love playing cricket because it keeps me active, improves my teamwork, and is a lot of fun!
